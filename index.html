History of the Creation

Ionut Sandu | Game Designer | Father | Crypto Enthusiast

From Vision to Venture:
Backed by VCs and Crypto.

It all started with an ordinary family evening. My son, Robert, and I were watching Stranger Things, and his childhood imagination sparked a brilliant idea: "What if these same heroes faced off against Jason Voorhees or Nosferatu in a new season?"
That's when it hit me. One core team—different episodes of horror. This is the very magic that not only my son craves but players around the world desire. It was the idea I had been dreaming of for so long without even realizing it. Different settings and different kinds of horror within a single ecosystem.
I looked at the current state of Web3 and saw a sea of projects where money comes first, not the game. At ODC Studio, we decided it was time to put an end to this. "Judas" is our answer. It's a world of photorealistic horror with a dizzying plot, where you can truly prove yourself as a gamer. The ability to earn will be a pleasant bonus for your skills, not a tedious grind.
When I shared this vision, familiar investors and exchanges supported it without hesitation. And so, our studio ODC and our pride—the game "Judas"—were born. We are not just creating another project. We are building the very future of Web3 where we ourselves would want to play.

Yours,
Ionut Sandu
