SEASON 2
"PERFORMANCE" SCREAMS UNDER THE TENTS

Scream, spectators! Your entrance onto the Arena of Blood is announced.

Forget everything you knew about horror and the first season. From now on, you are not forced to hide in corners and run away at the sound of creaking. Welcome to "The Performance" — the first bloody chapter of "Judas", where the circus is not a place for fun, but an endless nightmare, and applause merges with the crunch of bones.

What awaits behind the curtains of this madness:

  • A horde of circus freaks: Fight your way through swarms of psycho clowns with chainsaws for hands, agile acrobats whose bodies are twisted at unimaginable angles, and the enigmatic Magician who tears reality apart before your very eyes. Every enemy is part of the show, and their death must be spectacular.
  • A bloody circus arsenal: Your tools for survival range from an old cleaver to firearms with circus runes carved into the cartridges. Use the interactive environment: launch an enemy into the blade of a circus saw, drop a massive hammer on them, or lure them into a cage with a ferocious mutant predator.
  • Gameplay Feature: "The Price of Applause" The more spectacularly and brutally you deal with your enemies, the louder the "Arena's Approval" roars. Fill the meter to unleash the "Finale Performance" — a unique cinematic attack that instantly annihilates all enemies in the area with maximum brutality. But remember: the arena craves spectacle, and if you remain passive, the horrors will only grow stronger.
  • Storyline Mystique: The "Eternal Laughter" Circus is a living organism that feeds on fear and pain. Unravel its secrets, find notes from previous victims, and fulfill twisted "contracts" with otherworldly forces to gain new abilities and break through to the chapter's main antagonist — The Ringmaster, the puppet master of this hell.

Become part of the madness! Hurry to participate in the S1 alpha test and decide the fate of newcomers in S2.

The most active testers will receive the exclusive title "First Blood on the Arena" and the opportunity to influence what horrors await newcomers in the next season.

The show is about to begin. Are you ready for your entrance?
